Origin Story

Born from the crash,
built for the chain.

2008 — The Seed

The banks broke our family

Scottie watched the financial system gut her family's savings — money handed to strangers in suits who gambled with it and walked away clean. That rage never left her.

2017 — The Rabbit Hole

First Bitcoin purchase

Fell down the crypto rabbit hole while teaching herself to code. Realized blockchain wasn't just currency — it was an exit ramp from the rigged game entirely.

2021 — The Community

NFT summer changed everything

Minted her first NFT, joined the DeFi wave, and found her people. Not traders. Not suits. Builders and believers who'd rather own the protocol than trust the platform.

Building the community she wished existed when she started — zero corporate filter, all the conviction, and a front-row seat to the decentralized future.
